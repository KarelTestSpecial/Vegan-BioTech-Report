# Vegan BioTech Report – Edition 29 Sep 2025

This week, the vegan biotech sector saw significant advancements in both food and materials, with major companies securing funding and introducing groundbreaking innovations. From plant-based meat to biofabricated leather, the industry continues to push the boundaries of sustainability.

*   **Impossible Foods** (Impact: 9) – 01 Aug 2025 – [Impossible Foods raises $500 million to expand its plant-based meat production, reinforcing its position as a leader in the alternative protein market.](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2025-08-01/impossible-foods-secures-500-million-in-funding-for-plant-based-meat)
*   **Perfect Day** (Impact: 9) – 15 Sep 2025 – [Perfect Day acquires Sterling Biotech to enhance its precision-fermented dairy ingredients, marking a strategic move in the dairy alternative space.](https://www.perfectdayfoods.com/press/perfect-day-acquires-sterling-biotech)
*   **Memphis Meats** (Impact: 8) – 15 Jul 2025 – [Memphis Meats achieves a breakthrough in cell-based seafood, offering a sustainable and ethical alternative to traditional seafood.](https://www.memphismeats.com/news/memphis-meats-announces-cell-based-seafood-breakthrough)
*   **Bolt Threads** (Impact: 8) – 15 Aug 2025 – [Bolt Threads secures funding to scale up production of Mylo, its innovative mushroom-based leather alternative, furthering sustainable fashion.](https://www.boltthreads.com/news/bolt-threads-secures-funding-for-mylo-mushroom-leather)
*   **Modern Meadow** (Impact: 8) – 01 Sep 2025 – [Modern Meadow introduces Zoa, a biofabricated leather made from cellular agriculture, revolutionizing the leather industry with eco-friendly materials.](https://www.modernmeadow.com/news/modern-meadow-unveils-zoa-a-biofabricated-leather)
*   **MycoWorks** (Impact: 8) – 20 Sep 2025 – [MycoWorks raises funds to expand production of Reishi, its sustainable mushroom-based leather, contributing to a greener future in fashion.](https://www.google.com/search?q=MycoWorks+mushroom+leather+funding)

*   **Bio-on** (Impact: 7) – 20 Jul 2025 – [Bio-on develops a biodegradable plastic from food waste, addressing plastic pollution and promoting a circular economy.](https://www.bio-on.com/en/news/bio-on-announces-bioplastic-made-from-food-waste)

As the vegan biotech industry continues to innovate, these developments highlight the growing momentum towards sustainable and ethical alternatives in both food and materials. Stay tuned for more exciting updates as we move towards a greener future.
