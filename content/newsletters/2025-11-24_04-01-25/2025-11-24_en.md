# Vegan BioTech Report – Edition 24 Nov 2025

This week's Vegan BioTech Report highlights significant advancements in sustainable food and materials. From the opening of the first commercial Solein plant to major investments in bio-based cement and textiles, the industry is demonstrating remarkable progress in scaling up innovative vegan technologies.

## Top Stories This Week

*   **Solar Foods** (Impact: 9) – 01 Nov 2025 – Solar Foods has officially launched its inaugural commercial facility dedicated to the production of Solein, a novel protein source synthesized from air and electricity. [Link](https://www.google.com/search?q=Solar+Foods+commercial+Solein+plant+opens)
*   **BioMason** (Impact: 9) – 28 Sep 2025 – BioMason has successfully obtained substantial funding to accelerate the production of their environmentally friendly, biologically cultivated cement alternative for use in the construction sector. [Link](https://www.google.com/search?q=BioMason+investment+sustainable+cement)
*   **Modern Synthesis** (Impact: 8) – 16 Oct 2025 – Modern Synthesis has raised $4.1 million to further develop its microbial textile platform, allowing for expanded production of sustainable bio-fabricated materials. [Link](https://www.google.com/search?q=Modern+Synthesis+microbial+textile+platform+funding)
*   **The Every Company** (Impact: 8) – 15 Nov 2025 – The Every Company is partnering with several restaurant chains to offer its innovative animal-free egg white alternative, making it more readily available to consumers. [Link](https://www.google.com/search?q=The+Every+Company+restaurant+chains+egg+white)
*   **Change Foods** (Impact: 7) – 27 Oct 2025 – Change Foods has received new funding to enhance its precision-fermentation process for creating animal-free dairy products, paving the way for its commercial debut. [Link](https://www.google.com/search?q=Change+Foods+funding+animal-free+dairy)
*   **Bolt Threads** (Impact: 7) – 10 Sep 2025 – Bolt Threads is expanding its production capabilities for Mylo, its innovative mushroom-based leather alternative, in response to increasing market demand. [Link](https://www.google.com/search?q=Bolt+Threads+Mylo+production+expansion)

This week marks a period of significant growth and investment in the vegan biotech sector, particularly in scaling production and expanding market reach. Keep an eye on the adoption rates of these new materials and food alternatives as they become more widely available.
