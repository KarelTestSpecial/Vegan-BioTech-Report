# Vegan BioTech Report – Edition 10 Nov 2025

This week's Vegan BioTech Report highlights significant advancements in sustainable materials and animal-free food production. From luxury partnerships for mushroom leather to funding for precision-fermented dairy, the industry is rapidly scaling and gaining traction in diverse markets.

*   **Bolt Threads** (Impact: 9) – 01 Sep 2025 – Bolt Threads' Mylo, a mushroom-based leather alternative, is set to feature in a new luxury brand collaboration, marking a major step for sustainable materials in high-end fashion. *   **Desserto** (Impact: 7) – 05 Oct 2025 – Desserto's cactus leather is now being used in the interiors of a new range of electric vehicles, demonstrating the growing demand for sustainable materials in the automotive sector. [Link](https://www.google.com/search?q=Desserto+cactus+leather+automotive+interiors)

This week underscored the increasing adoption of vegan biotechnology solutions across multiple sectors. Keep an eye on further developments in precision fermentation and bio-fabricated materials as companies scale production and expand into new markets.
