# Vegan BioTech Report – Edition 05 Dec 2025

This week's Vegan BioTech Report highlights major advancements in alternative proteins and sustainable materials. Several companies are scaling up production of animal-free foods and textiles, marking a significant step towards a more sustainable future.

## Top Stories This Week

*   **Solar Foods** (Impact: 9) – 01 Oct 2025 – Solar Foods has commenced operations at its first commercial facility in Finland, producing Solein, a novel protein created from air and electricity through gas fermentation. [Link](https://www.google.com/search?q=Solar+Foods+factory+Finland+operations)
*   **The Every Company** (Impact: 9) – 18 Sep 2025 – The Every Company has launched its commercially available egg protein, produced without animals via precision fermentation, and is aimed at revolutionizing the food industry. [Link](https://www.google.com/search?q=The+Every+Co.+launches+animal-free+egg+protein)
*   **Modern Synthesis** (Impact: 8) – 28 Oct 2025 – Modern Synthesis has secured funding to further develop its microbial textile platform, which uses microbes to create sustainable textiles, offering a replacement for traditional fabrics. [Link](https://www.google.com/search?q=Modern+Synthesis+funding+microbial+textile+platform)
*   **MycoWorks** (Impact: 8) – 10 Oct 2025 – MycoWorks is expanding its production capacity of Reishi, their innovative leather alternative made from mycelium, to satisfy increasing market demand. [Link](https://www.google.com/search?q=MycoWorks+expands+Reishi+production+capacity)
*   **Change Foods** (Impact: 7) – 15 Nov 2025 – Change Foods has received funding to accelerate the scaling of its precision fermentation process for producing animal-free dairy proteins. [Link](https://www.google.com/search?q=Change+Foods+secures+funding+animal-free+dairy)
*   **Remilk** (Impact: 7) – 05 Nov 2025 – Remilk has announced plans to construct a new, large-scale production facility in North America to produce its dairy products made through precision fermentation. [Link](https://www.google.com/search?q=Remilk+new+production+facility+North+America)
*   **Genomatica** (Impact: 7) – 22 Nov 2025 – Genomatica has announced that it has developed a bio-based alternative to nylon, using fermentation processes to create the innovative material. [Link](https://www.google.com/search?q=Genomatica+bio-based+nylon+alternative)

This week's developments highlight the rapid growth and increasing sophistication of the vegan biotech sector. We anticipate continued innovation and expansion in both alternative proteins and sustainable materials in the coming months.
