# Vegan BioTech Report – Edition 08 Sep 2025

This week's Vegan BioTech Report highlights major advancements in sustainable food and materials. From vegan dairy collaborations to mushroom leather production, the industry continues to innovate and expand.

## Top Stories This Week

*   **Perfect Day** (Impact: 10) – 01 Jul 2025 – Perfect Day has partnered with Nestle to develop and launch a new line of vegan dairy ice cream, utilizing their innovative whey protein. [Link](https://www.google.com/search?q=Perfect+Day+Nestle+vegan+dairy+ice_cream)
*   **Bolt Threads** (Impact: 9) – 15 Jun 2025 – Bolt Threads is ramping up production of their Mylo mushroom leather to meet the increasing demand from fashion brands seeking eco-friendly alternatives. [Link](https://www.google.com/search?q=Bolt+Threads+Mylo+mushroom+leather+production)
*   **The Every Co.** (Impact: 9) – 18 May 2025 – The Every Co. has received FDA approval for their groundbreaking egg white protein, paving the way for its use in a wider range of food products. [Link](https://www.google.com/search?q=The+Every+Co+FDA+approval+egg+white+protein)
*   **Change Foods** (Impact: 8) – 20 Jul 2025 – Change Foods has successfully produced dairy-identical vegan cheese using precision fermentation, replicating the protein structure of cow's milk. [Link](https://www.google.com/search?q=Change+Foods+dairy+identical+cheese+precision_fermentation)
*   **Biomason** (Impact: 8) – 28 Jul 2025 – Biomason is expanding their production of bio-based cement alternatives, using microorganisms to grow cement and significantly reduce CO2 emissions. [Link](https://www.google.com/search?q=Biomason+bio_based+cement+alternative+expansion)
*   **Solar Foods** (Impact: 7) – 28 May 2025 – Solar Foods is increasing production of their Solein protein, showcasing the potential of creating protein from air through innovative fermentation techniques. [Link](https://www.google.com/search?q=Solar+Foods+Solein+protein+air_fermentation+expansion)
*   **Algiknit** (Impact: 7) – 10 May 2025 – Algiknit has entered into a partnership to develop seaweed-based yarns for use in the apparel industry, providing a sustainable alternative to conventional textiles. [Link](https://www.google.com/search?q=Algiknit+seaweed+yarns+apparel_partnership)
*   **Mango Materials** (Impact: 7) – 01 Aug 2025 – Mango Materials has announced the construction of a new facility dedicated to PHA production using methane, creating biodegradable plastic alternatives from a potent greenhouse gas. [Link](https://www.google.com/search?q=Mango+Materials+PHA+production+methane_facility)

This week demonstrated significant progress in both food and materials, with companies scaling up production and securing key partnerships. Keep an eye on further developments in precision fermentation and bio-based material innovation as the industry continues to evolve.
