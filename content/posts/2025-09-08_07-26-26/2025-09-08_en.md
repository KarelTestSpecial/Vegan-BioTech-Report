# Vegan BioTech Report – Edition 08 Sep 2025

This week's Vegan BioTech Report showcases exciting advancements in sustainable food and materials. From air-based protein to mycelium leather, companies are scaling up innovative vegan solutions.

## Top Stories This Week

*   **Solar Foods** (Impact: 9) – 28 Jun 2025 – Solar Foods has officially opened its Finnish factory, commencing production of Solein, a protein created from air and electricity. [Link](https://www.google.com/search?q=Solar+Foods+Solein+protein+factory+Finland)
*   **Perfect Day** (Impact: 9) – 01 Jul 2025 – Perfect Day is collaborating with Nestle to launch a new vegan chocolate milk made using Perfect Day's animal-free whey protein. [Link](https://www.google.com/search?q=Perfect+Day+Nestle+vegan+chocolate+milk)
*   **Ananas Anam** (Impact: 8) – 10 Jun 2025 – Ananas Anam's Piñatex, a pineapple leaf fiber-based leather alternative, is featured in a new sustainable collection by H&M. [Link](https://www.google.com/search?q=Pinatex+Ananas+Anam+H%26M+sustainable+collection)
*   **MycoWorks** (Impact: 8) – 28 Aug 2025 – MycoWorks has inaugurated a new production facility in South Carolina dedicated to producing Reishi, their mycelium-based leather alternative. [Link](https://www.google.com/search?q=MycoWorks+Reishi+production+facility+South+Carolina)
*   **Change Foods** (Impact: 7) – 21 Aug 2025 – Change Foods has secured $12 million in funding to scale up its precision fermentation technology for producing animal-free cheese alternatives. [Link](https://www.google.com/search?q=Change+Foods+$12M+precision+fermentation+cheese)
*   **Algiknit** (Impact: 7) – 22 Jul 2025 – Algiknit completed a Series A funding round to expand the production of their algae-based yarn for the fashion industry. [Link](https://www.google.com/search?q=Algiknit+Series+A+algae-based+yarn)
*   **Air Protein** (Impact: 7) – 18 Jun 2025 – Air Protein has been granted Generally Recognized As Safe (GRAS) status, facilitating the commercialization of their innovative air-based protein. [Link](https://www.google.com/search?q=Air+Protein+GRAS+status)

This week saw major milestones for companies developing novel vegan foods and materials, including factory openings, major partnerships, and key regulatory approvals. Keep an eye on further developments in precision fermentation and sustainable materials as these technologies continue to mature.
