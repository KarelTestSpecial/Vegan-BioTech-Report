# Vegan BioTech Report – Edition 01 Sep 2025

This week's Vegan BioTech Report highlights significant advancements in sustainable materials and food production. From vegan dairy collaborations to innovative bio-based materials, the industry is rapidly evolving towards a more sustainable future.

## Top Stories This Week

*   **Perfect Day** (Impact: 10) – 01 Jul 2025 – Perfect Day has partnered with Nestle to develop and launch a new line of vegan dairy ice cream, bringing their innovative whey protein to a wider consumer base. [Link](https://www.google.com/search?q=Perfect+Day+Nestle+vegan+dairy+ice_cream)
*   **Bolt Threads** (Impact: 9) – 15 Jun 2025 – Bolt Threads is scaling up production of their Mylo mushroom leather, allowing fashion brands to embrace a sustainable and ethical alternative to traditional leather. [Link](https://www.google.com/search?q=Bolt+Threads+Mylo+mushroom+leather+production)
*   **The Every Co.** (Impact: 9) – 18 May 2025 – The Every Co. has received FDA approval for their innovative egg white protein, paving the way for its expanded use in the food industry. [Link](https://www.google.com/search?q=The+Every+Co+FDA+approval+egg+white+protein)
*   **Change Foods** (Impact: 8) – 20 Jul 2025 – Change Foods has successfully produced dairy-identical vegan cheese using precision fermentation, replicating the protein structure of cow's milk without animal involvement. [Link](https://www.google.com/search?q=Change+Foods+dairy+identical+cheese+precision_fermentation)
*   **Biomason** (Impact: 8) – 28 Jul 2025 – Biomason is expanding production of their bio-based cement alternative, reducing CO2 emissions by growing cement with microorganisms. [Link](https://www.google.com/search?q=Biomason+bio_based+cement+alternative+expansion)
*   **Solar Foods** (Impact: 7) – 28 May 2025 – Solar Foods is expanding its production of Solein protein, demonstrating the potential of creating protein from air using fermentation. [Link](https://www.google.com/search?q=Solar+Foods+Solein+protein+air_fermentation+expansion)
*   **Algiknit** (Impact: 7) – 10 May 2025 – Algiknit has partnered to develop seaweed-based yarns for apparel, providing a sustainable alternative to conventional textiles. [Link](https://www.google.com/search?q=Algiknit+seaweed+yarns+apparel+partnership)
*   **Mango Materials** (Impact: 7) – 01 Aug 2025 – Mango Materials is opening a new facility for PHA production, converting methane into biodegradable plastic alternatives. [Link](https://www.google.com/search?q=Mango+Materials+PHA+production+methane+facility)

This week showcased significant progress in both food and materials sectors, with companies scaling up production and securing key partnerships. Keep an eye on regulatory approvals and further advancements in fermentation technology, as these will be crucial for the continued growth of the vegan biotech industry.
